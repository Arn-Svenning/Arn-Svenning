## Featured projects

### BetweenShifts — Commercial mobile application

BetweenShifts is a cross platform community and wellbeing app designed for
healthcare professionals.

I am independently developing the product and am responsible for its product
direction, application architecture, implementation, testing, CI/CD and release
preparation.

**Selected engineering areas:** feature module based architecture, authenticated data

**Status:** Preparing for commercial release.

### The Raft — Bachelor's thesis project

A Unity game developed for my bachelor's thesis:

I developed the core gameplay systems, designed the software architecture and

### Chained 2 Violence — Eight-person Steam project

Chained 2 Violence is a local co-op action roguelike in which players are
connected by an unbreakable chain and fight through waves of enemies across
evolving arenas.

The game was developed by an eight person team and prepared for public release
on Steam. It is the largest collaborative game project I have worked on so far.

I was responsible for much of the project's underlying architecture and core
gameplay functionality, including the combat system, animation system, chain
mechanics and local two player functionality.

**Technologies:** C# and Unity

**Status:** Unreleased. The Steam store page is live while the team evaluates
the remaining stability work before deciding on release.

[View on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3307300/Chained_2_Violence/)
